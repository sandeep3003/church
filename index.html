Welcome to peniel Gospel Fellowship

By God’s command as per the will of Lord, On 7th June 1978, man of God Dr. K. Nireekshan Paul has established this Peniel Gospel Fellowship in Eluru – West Godavari district. May the glory be to Lord Jesus Christ alone for the grace He has given to spread this ministry to mulitple centres with a burden for souls. Amen.

In succession Pastor D. Vijay Kumar and Brother D. Ravi Kumar have been given a vision to establish a centre for P. G. F in Bengaluru in 2011 May first week . This vision is for saving the souls in the region of munnekolala and eventually lead many souls in the surrounding regions of Karnataka to Lord.

The promise given by Lord was – Joshua 1:3 (NASB) Every place on which the sole of your foot treads, I have given it to you, just as I spoke to Moses.

In this order with the great support given by Brother D. Ravi Kumar and other church members we’re proceeding as a fellowship only with our eyes set on our savior Lord Jesus Christ. This is a fellowship only founded on faith. For all our needs we look unto God alone. Till now God Himself has been leading us through many needs providing from His riches. May Glory and Honor belong to God alone.
